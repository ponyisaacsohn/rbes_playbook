Social Arcology Lab (SAL)

SAL is the architecture and urban-planning wing of RBES, founded 2016 in Shanghai as part of the Dawn House project.

The Mission

SAL designs infrastructures for the Infinite Timeline—spaces that embody continuity and shared intelligence.

Current Focus – Infinite Thailand Vision

Nine associated projects:

  1. Bangkok Tunnelparks 2. The National Boardwalk 3. The Green Loop 4. The Dome Zone
  2. The Silom Arcology 6. Chinatown 3.0 7. RiFirenze 8. NeoAthens 9. Bang Kachao Bolo

The Method

Workshops mix architecture, storytelling, and systems design.
English is the lingua franca for drawings, proposals, and presentations.

The Goal

To manifest the Infinite Timeline through real, buildable forms—cities that learn.
