Unicornworld Imagineering

Unicornworld Imagineering builds walk-in experiences where imagined worlds become places.

The Myth

Inside the Unicornworld Saga, the STAR unicorn Nichlolas lives in a chemically induced utopia—an endless, televised paradise owned by the Moraine Family Trust.
The audience watches Nichlolas sing the Great Morning Song, invent new foods, and DJ waterfalls—never realizing the world is a zoo-show.
The saga follows Nichlolas’ journey to find the real Unicornworld.

The Studio

For youth ~17–18 who want to design worlds you can walk through.
Workshops focus on spatial storytelling and experiential architecture.
Year-one project: a mall-scale photo/experience set where visitors invent and photograph their own characters and worlds.

The Practice

Students act as Imagineers: storyboards, environment sketches, small-scale fabrication.
English is used for pitching, documentation, and collaboration—English as design software.

The Future

Long-term vision: a real theme park combining zones from the Unicornworld Saga, Rulebreakers Cycle, and FafaBaby Worlds.
