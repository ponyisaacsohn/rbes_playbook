Meanbabies Management

Meanbabies Management operates as a fictional talent-management company training artists to manage both themselves and others.

The Myth

The Meanbabies first appear in the Rulebreakers Saga as tiny, furious creatures carried by Holy Sloths—each one a parody of ego, fear, and control.
Across the polyverse they kidnap heroes and must be rescued or integrated.

The Studio

In RBES, the studio helps participants work with their inner Meanbabies—the parts that block collaboration or creation.
Through a system called Triadic Management, every participant is both manager and managed.
Workshops simulate a functioning agency producing content from the Rulebreakers, FafaBaby, and StarKid universes.

The Practice

Each project begins with role assignment and negotiation sessions:
artists wear “Meanbaby hats” or glasses to safely act out critical or possessive instincts.
Teams then co-produce short works—videos, stories, prototypes—learning to convert friction into production.

The Goal

Meanbabies Management teaches how to transform conflict into creative energy.
When the studio eventually connects to the public, it will package cross-world characters and products emerging from RBES.
