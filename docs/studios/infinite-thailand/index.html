Infinite Thailand Strategies (ITS)

Infinite Thailand Strategies is RBES’s consulting and research studio — connecting Thailand’s finite industries to the Infinite Timeline.

The Concept

The Finite Timeline ends; the Infinite Timeline continues.
ITS aligns tourism, food, hospitality, and property development with infinite ethics and design.

Hypothetical Clients

  1. Tourism Authority of Thailand – cultural infrastructure and sustainable travel
  2. Major Food Company – scaling a Thai brand internationally
  3. Experience Worker Lodges – a new hospitality model
  4. Tunnelpark Property Group – developing the land around Bangkok’s Tunnelparks

The Practice

Workshops simulate real consulting firms: briefs, presentations, strategy decks, and negotiations — all in English.
Teams analyze, design, and pitch Infinite-aligned business models.

The Goal

To demonstrate how policy, enterprise, and imagination can coexist on an infinite timeline —
turning speculative consulting into real, ethical development.
