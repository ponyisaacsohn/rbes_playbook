English Is Money Gaming

A studio exploring how games can teach language, economy, and collaboration all at once.

The Three Operational Ideas

  1. Gamify the Studios – each RBES studio becomes a game (FarmVille for Imagineering).
  2. English = Money Circuit – players earn for fluency and participation.
  3. Enterprise Licensing – partner with large companies needing English training software.

The Method

Workshops prototype board games, apps, and simulations linking language learning to value creation.

The Context

Developed with Infinite Thailand Strategies, it operates between finite incentive structures and infinite education.

The Goal

To turn learning English into an economy of meaning and reward.
