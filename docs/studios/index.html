The Studios

Rulebreakers English Studios are built around institutional performance art — make-believe made real.
Each studio explores a different creative-economic form where English is not just learned, but performed.

  • Unicornworld Imagineering — design theme parks, future environments, and dream infrastructures.
  • Meanbabies Management — manage imaginary pop idols, hybrids, and conceptual celebrities.
  • Clownpump Press — run the Rulebreakers newspaper, magazine, and media works.
  • Infinite Thailand Solutions — design strategies for the future Thai miracle.
  • Channel 99 — produce TV shows, sitcoms, and performance-based series.
  • Experience Worker — create new tourism, lifestyle, and experiential media.
  • Social Arcology Lab — prototype urban futures and ecological city designs.
  • English Is Money Gaming — play and design incentive-based learning games.
  • TMT Bottega — make transpersonal paintings that merge consciousness and commerce.
