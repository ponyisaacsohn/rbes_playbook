Rulebreakers English Studios is a startup disguised as a school disguised as a startup.
USP: English via Institutional Performance Art.

Instead of lectures, students design worlds in the Unicornworld Imagineering Studio.
Instead of teachers, they co-write animated films with their peers in Mean Babies Animation Lab.
Instead of homework, they publish newspapers in Experience Worker Media.
Instead of business classes, they research the perfect Pad Thai at the Pad Thai Optimization Bureau.
Instead of architecture theory, they draw tunnelparks in Tunnelpark Architecture.
Instead of conversation drills, they host live interviews on Channel 99.
Instead of logic puzzles, they design games in the Game Economy Studio.
Instead of sociology essays, they simulate future cities in Social Arcology.
Instead of marketing exercises, they build brands inside TMT Bottega.

This is The Plan for turning that idea into reality in three moves —
Workshops, Year-Long Test of Concept, and Infinite Thailand.

It’s written for student-owners, teachers, workshop collaborators, and angel investors —
those who will build the studios, teach inside them, collaborate on their workshops, and fund their proof of concept.
